WELCOME!



Concerned about your weight or potential for developing diabetes? This website offers a preliminary risk assessment to help you understand your individual situation. What We Offer By answering a few simple questions about your lifestyle habits, family history, and basic health information, you'll receive an initial evaluation of your risk for both obesity and type 2 diabetes. Important to Remember: This tool is for informational purposes only and cannot diagnose any medical condition. We strongly recommend consulting a healthcare professional for a complete evaluation and personalized plan. Benefits of this Assessment: Gain insights: Understand the factors that might influence your risk for obesity and diabetes. Empowerment: Be motivated to take proactive steps towards a healthier lifestyle. Early Detection: Identify potential risk factors early for better management. What We Can't Do: Provide medical diagnoses or treatment plans. Replace a doctor's consultation and personalized advice. Taking Action for Your Wellbeing: If your assessment indicates an elevated risk for either condition, we highly recommend scheduling an appointment with your doctor. They can provide a comprehensive evaluation, discuss potential prevention strategies, or recommend treatment options if necessary. Remember: Early intervention is crucial. By making positive lifestyle changes today, you can significantly reduce your risk for both obesity and type 2 diabetes, leading to a healthier future. Let's get started on your journey to a healthier you!
